About the Book

A Clear Autumn Night

Two young field mice refuse to sleep when there are stars to look at. While the others retreat into their burrows, Wim and Frida head out into the autumn night and into an adventure full of light and wonder. Published by Baeschlin Verlag in September 2025, the book was the season's favourite project at the publisher.

The Process

From First Draft to Finished Book

During character design, differing visions emerged between myself, the author, and the publisher. I sought out the conversation early: asking questions, listening carefully, and building a shared understanding of the visual language they had in mind.
