What are Visual Sketchnotes?

Making Ideas Visible

Sketchnoting means listening, processing, and visually capturing information simultaneously in real time. The result is not a polished design but a living document that makes the core of an idea graspable instantly.

✦

Identify the 2 or 3 ideas worth anchoring

✦

✦

What I learned

✦

Rapid filtering: capturing key points quickly and deciding what matters in the moment

✦

Visual translation: turning abstract ideas into concrete, readable images

✦

Structure under pressure: condensing dense content into something clear at a glance
